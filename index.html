About me

Who

I am

Hi, I'm Steve Jones

I'm a qualified teacher of Computer Science and a self-taught web developer, among other things. I live in Cambridgeshire with my wife, three-year-old twins and Staffie, Ripley.

I have an English degree and professional experience in both web and print media. My career in education was born out of my own love and passion for learning and self-development.

What

I do

Creative thinking

I have professional working and teaching knowledge of numerous web platforms, languages and software. Click the icons to read more.
I use HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript to create dynamic, interactive and intuitive web content. I work with Jade, SASS, jQuery and a range of different CSS and JS frameworks to develop with speed and efficiency.
I develop responsive designs that provide an appropriate and consistent user experience across all different screen sizes, devices and browsers with an emphasis on clear content, clean presentation and accessibility. Try resizing the browser ;-)
Working with industry-leading software including Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign, I create and edit everything from photographs, icons and logos to animations, videos and audio. With a background in copywriting and print media I also produce professional posters, leaflets, business cards and more.
